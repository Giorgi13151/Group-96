🎤 Who is EsDeeKid?🎤



EsDeeKid is a British underground rapper from Liverpool, England who started gaining major attention around 2024–2026.


He’s known for:

Wearing a balaclava (mask) and hiding his identity Never revealing his real name, age, or face A strong Liverpool (Scouse) accent Growing popularity on YouTube, TikTok, and Spotify 🚀 Rise to Fame

He began releasing music around 2023–2024, mainly online. Some early and popular songs include:

“Bally” (feat. Rico Ace) “LV Sandals” “4 Raws” “Phantom” (his biggest hit)

👉 “Phantom” became extremely popular and gained huge streaming numbers, helping him go viral. By 2025, he released his debut album:

🎵 “Rebel” (2025) Charted internationally Reached the Billboard 200 🎧 Music Style

His music fits into modern UK rap styles, including: Cloud rap Trap Atmospheric / dark beats

Key features:
Heavy bass and distorted sound Melodic, sometimes slurred delivery Dark, moody vibe

His lyrics often cover:
Fashion Nightlife Street themes Relationships 😷 Mysterious Identity

A big part of his image is mystery:
Always wears a mask Doesn’t reveal personal details Avoids interviews
This has made fans very curious and helped him stand out.
🤯 Viral Theory (Timothée Chalamet)

Reasons:
